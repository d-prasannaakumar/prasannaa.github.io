About Me

I am Prasannaa from Coimbatore, India.

I'm a Maker and I have been continuously developing skills in IoT, 3D Printing, Fabrication, Machine Learning and Robotics. I have worked on many projects and received fundings from private institutions as well as government agencies. I'm looking forward to creating something that makes our society sustainable.

Nature is the best innovation among all. I understand Research and Development through daily activities. I believe that science is interdisciplinary and cannot be categorized. My interests include Product development, R&D, 3D Printing and Sustainable practices. Collecting Betta fish is my most favorite hobby. I collect a lot of betta fishes from countries like Thailand, Vietnam and Indonesia. 

Innovation isn't about inventing new things. It's all about making things work better.

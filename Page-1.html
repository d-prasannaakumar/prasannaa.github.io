FACE SHIELD FOR FRONTLINE WORKERS

During this pandemic, I designed a low-cost face shield with just 3 materials. I and my friend Arrenius designed a low-cost face shield which is fog proof and delivered it to a few hospitals in the city. We used just 3 things which made its cost around 35Rs. We used an acrylic glass, OHP sheet and 3D Printed rivets.

ANTIBIOTICS DETECTION IN MILK

Recently the government passed a GO for all diary industries to evaluate the content of antibiotics in milk which is dangerous when exceeded. Also, to perform one test it costs much. I designed a portable device that can measure the antibiotics level using photo electronics.

ML FOR SEGREGATION OF TOMATOES INTO GRADES 

Tomato is one of the major crops in Tamil Nadu. But still, farmers are not able to find the appropriate profit from it. I and My friend Haritha worked on a project. We used a color sensor and Particle Argon to segregate tomatoes into Grades  ( Grade A, Grade B and Grade C) using Machine Learning. I used TensorFlow Lite so that I can use a microcontroller instead of a microprocessor. 

DIGITAL WIRELESS SPECTROSCOPY

Digital Wireless Spectroscopy is one of its first kind of handheld spectrometer which can work in different bands. A spectrometer is an equipment which is used in Food, Medicine, Space, Quality Check, Research and Production fields. This particular device is very small so that it could be handheld This Wireless Digital Spectrometer detects wavelengths in the visible range at 610, 680, 730, 760, 810 and 860nm of light [18 frequencies of light-sensing from 410nm to 940nm optional], each with 20nm of full-width half-max detection. One of the best features is that this device is integrated with 405nm UV, 5700k White, and 875nm IR LEDs which makes it independent for external light sources! This project was selected for presentation in India International Science Festival (IISF) 2019 under Young Scientists Conclave for Poster Presentation.

LASER POWER METER

In SRM University, a research project funded by DST is done to study the properties of Ferromagnetic particles when exposed to high power laser. To measure the power of the laser, a laser power meter is needed. But the meters available in markets are too costly for its specification. I developed a low-cost Laser Power Meter which can be used for high power lasers. [ SRM University, Chennai - Photonics Lab ]

SOLAR INVERTER MONITORING SYSTEM

A solar plant in Coimbatore of 3MW power capacity which is used by a textile mill. They reached me for developing a remote monitoring system to monitor the power generation. I and my friend Subash developed a remote monitoring system connecting the 3 inverters ( ABB and Bonfiglioli ) using RS485 which can be accessed from their office which is 56 kilometers away from the plant.

IoT BASED AUTOMATED POWER SUPPLY

The same lab from SRM reached me out for building an automatic power supply. I converted an existing manual power supply by additionally adding my custom device to make it remote access and control the gauss (Magnetic Field) produced by the electromagnet.

BREAKOUT BOARD FOR RFM69 - LoRa

I designed and developed a breakout board for RFM69CW which is a LoRa module. The board for designed in eagle and I made the custom board with a PCB clad and etching solution. 

LOCAL WEATHER FORECAST DEVICE

Weather forecasts are made out of a central station. But what farmers require is a local weather forecast. I designed and developed a local weather forecast station that a farmer can install on their own farm. The station we developed had icons and supported English, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada and Malayalam. We achieved an 86% perfect forecast for 3 days. This project was funded by the state government and was selected as the top 10th project in TNSI [ Tamil Nadu Students innovators ] Program.

CO TRACKER

The place where I live is nearby an industrial hub. Sometimes I could feel the pollution level increasing. So I decided to install a CO tracker than can track the Carbon Monoxide emission easily. I made it using Particle electron which works with GSM and connects to the cloud to push the data. The data was accessible in the android app. After a month of collecting data, I could see the emission levels. Also, I gave a proposal to the City Corporation for starting a Public addressing system to display the pollution levels in different parts of the city.

SMART STORAGE BIN

The idea of smart storage bin was from a friend of mine. She wanted to develop a post-harvest storage bin for micro farmers and make it easily affordable. I helped her on the technical side by building a prototype. The bin was installed with a heater for maintaining the temperature and humidity inside the bin.

SMART ENERGY METER

The Smart Energy Meter is a Pilot project by myself and a team of my juniors. Usually, Smart Energy Meters refers to monitoring the consumption of power. But we developed an algorithm to monitor the consumption as well as the wastage of power. Our algorithm runs 24x7 to monitor the usage and wastage. This project was funded by the college under the Seed Project Scheme and is still under progress. As of now we have installed devices in a complete block and are working perfectly. Soon our devices will be installed on the entire campus!

MENTORING FOR THULIR HYDROPONICS

Thulir hydroponics is a student Startup which provides fresh greens for families in the city. The startup was started in the year 2019. I worked with the team from scratch and helped them with the technical side.
